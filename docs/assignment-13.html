Final Project

  • What will it do?
    • It will produce coffee at the que of a person seating on the chair in the morning.
    A deeper look into it, It will be composed of two different systems:

      Chair System
    • 1.The first system will be embeded inside the chair. It will have a simple swithc to detect if something is on it. It will will later turn on a capacitive sensor where it will determine if there is a person or if its something lese like books or a bag.
    • 1.1 If its a person, it will make sure the time of day is morning, with an internal timer. It will also cheak if its the first time, if it is not it will do nothing.
    • 1.2 If all is true, then it will send a signal via bluetooth to the output (the coffee maker pcb).
    • 1.3 After sending the signal it will count the signal sent. In case the person sits again in the same time period it will not sent this again.
    • 1.4 15 min afer the signal is sent the chair system will signal to the human that the coffee should be ready. (not sure by what means yet.)
      Coffee System
    • 2. The system starts with a signal from the "Chair system" Where system 1 sends a signal via bluetooth to make coffee.
    • 2.1 The system will in turn send a signal from the buetooth to the Attiny 45 to turn on the relay.
    • 2.2 The Attiny will turn the relay with a 5v charge.
    • 2.3 The Relay (already hacked into the coffee machine) will turn the drip coffee machine on.
    • 2.4 The machine will take about 15 min to make coffee.
