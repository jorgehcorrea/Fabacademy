    This test was fully executed by my classmate Ale. We discussed certail concepts as a group, and at the execution time it seemed a better collaboration if everyione tackled a part of the tests and later shared the results.

    In this case, the top row was a variation of power, while the bottom row was a variation on speed.

    Press-fit Kit - Light bulb screen

    Press-fit kit concept was developed because I needed something to cover a couple of naked light bulbs in an extra room. The concept was to create something that provides a diffused light, that neatly wraps around a light bulb.

    The idea was to create this interlocking system of simple shapes that would morph into a larger organic shape that created into a screen around the light bulb and created a much nicer light.

    The shapes where intended snap between shapes creating the banana shaped lamp. It was hoverer filled with holes that let the light pass through and the circumference shape did not diminish as expected. It then made me re-evaluate the shapes capabilities and resulted into a experimentation resulting in the later lamp shapes.

    The snapping did not work exactly as I planned, but it was enough to create a finning around the light bulb. It was tight enough to hold its own weight and depending on the patten it also created an aesthetically pleasing shape in the day time.
