Int his case I belive the license I would use, that is congruent with my personal belives is the Attribution 4.0 International

From my perspective, if somebody wants to learn something they should. I think even with al this vast internet shared knowladge it is still hard to find specific specialised information and shareing it is the best way to move forawrd. So why to activly give the tools for it.

Its like if somebody asks for my help to learn something, I cant stop everything I'm doing to teach them but I'll give them pointer, tips and a path to follow and learn just as it has been given to me.

It becomes a case of reciprocity and I think taking on that resposability is a must in every scenario, where somebody shows the aptitude or intrest of actually wanting to learn. So I figure give it openly is best.

Imagining:

A Vacuum Coffee maker, using 2 500ml lab flasks, and having a direct connection to the water supply system. The bottom flask is heated to boil with an electric element. With a direct water supply system the coffee machine could automatically fill up with water and auto-clean it self.

Additionally a coffee reservoir could dispense grounded coffee powder via a screw device, placing new coffee every time.

I think there are enough users that could consider the coffee machine static appliance as is a sink or kitchen stoves.
